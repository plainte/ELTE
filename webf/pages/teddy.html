Park "Teddy" Jin-seong (Hangul: 박진성) is a League of Legends esports player, currently bot laner for T1. Known for having the highest CS rating in League of Legends history, with a score of 1456 creep kills, breaking Zven and Bang's record of 946 and and 972 respectively.

Considered as a highly rated player of Ezreal. Reached 1000 kills in the LCK on February 26, 2020.
